

## Punya's Portfolio Website

A single-page, dark-themed developer portfolio with a techy aesthetic — monospace accents, subtle code-inspired elements, and smooth scroll navigation.

### Sections

1. **Navbar** — Fixed top nav with smooth-scroll links to each section + your name as logo

2. **Hero** — Full-screen intro with your name, title "Senior UI Developer & Tech Lead", tagline from your profile summary, location, and CTA buttons (Contact / Download Resume). Subtle animated terminal-style or typing effect for the tagline.

3. **About** — Your profile summary with 7+ years experience highlights. Key stats displayed as cards (7+ Years, 2 Trading Platforms Built, Team Lead).

4. **Experience Timeline** — Vertical timeline showing your 3 roles:
   - Edelweiss Global Markets — AVP, Software Development (2022–Present)
   - Mindfire Solutions — Senior Software Engineer (2020–2022)
   - Infosys Ltd. — Systems Engineer (2018–2020)
   
   Each with key bullet points and tech tags.

5. **Key Achievements** — Highlighted cards for your standout accomplishments (algo trading platforms, low-code framework, legacy migration).

6. **Skills** — Categorized grid with tech icons/badges: Frontend, Backend, Languages, Databases & Messaging, DevOps, Testing, Architecture.

7. **Education** — B.Tech from Silicon Institute of Technology with CGPA.

8. **Contact** — Contact form + your email and phone, with social links.

### Design Details
- Dark background with subtle grid/dot pattern
- Accent color: Electric blue or cyan for highlights
- Monospace font for headings/code elements, clean sans-serif for body
- Smooth scroll animations on section entry
- Responsive for mobile and desktop
